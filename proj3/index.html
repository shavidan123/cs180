Mean Face from Danes Dataset

Using the Danes dataset, I computed the mean face by averaging keypoints and warping images to the mean shape, then taking the average of the morphed faces. Below are several faces from the dataset, morphed into the mean shape, and examples of warping my face into the average face's geometry and vice versa:

Conclusion

This project explored several techniques for facial keypoint selection, triangulation, affine warping, morphing, computing mean faces, and generating caricatures. These methods allow for powerful and creative image transformations.
